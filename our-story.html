Our Story

Our story started of as a dream, Declan has a passion for whiskey. His love for whiskey grew into the dream of one day owning a distillery. Declan travelled to many countrys, tasting different flavours from different cultures. Declan and his brother Jake worked very hard, experimenting with pros and cons of different whiskeys in perssue to make the best whiskey. The location was cruital because Declan and his brother thought that it was only right for the distillery to tell their story and show their hard work. The brothers grew up in Boyle in Co.Rosscommon, and have many fond memories in Boyle, this is why they deeemed it the best location to set up their business as this was the town in which made them.

Our dreams are now reality!

We have worked and tried and tested and now have 4 superb products for you to enjoy. Two wonderfully smooth Irish whiskeys, an amazing brandy and beautifully spiced rum.
