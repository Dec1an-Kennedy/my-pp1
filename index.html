Meet Declan

Meet our master distiller, Declan Kennedy (Deaglan Ó'Cinnéide). Declan is from Boyle, Co.Roscommon, and has a love for whiskey. Declan is 18 years old and is currently training to become a full stack developer. Declan lives in a family of 5 and is the middle child of 3. Declan has many hobbies such as cars, sport, coding and many more. Having a distillery in his home town of Boyle is a massive accoplishment and a dream come true for him. Despite his young age, he is very devoted to the whiskey industry and if focused on thriving his business.
